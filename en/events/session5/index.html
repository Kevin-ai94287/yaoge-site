AI Application Bootcamp Session 5: From Zero to One, Build an AI Application Ready for Pitch

July 2025

AI Application Bootcamp Session 5

From July 11 to 12, the 5th AI application bootcamp concluded successfully. Entrepreneurs from various industries participated with one goal: master AI tools in two days and build a deployable application.

Learn Tools, But Learn Scenarios

The training covered mainstream AI tools and coding basics, but the focus was on applying AI to one's own field. Participants brought real problems and found their AI use cases through hands-on guidance.

Conclusion: AI tools are the foundation, but scenario deployment is key.

From "Can't Do" to "Done"

Many participants previously thought AI had a high barrier to entry, but through two days of systematic learning, they built their own applications step by step.

Conclusion: With the right approach, anyone can build an AI application.

Pitch: From Idea to Product

The pitch session was the highlight. Participants demonstrated both technical implementation and the business logic behind their apps.

Conclusion: Only when you build it and present it have you truly learned.
