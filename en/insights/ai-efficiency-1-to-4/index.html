AI Efficiency Leap: 1.0 Manual to 4.0 AI Assistant

YaoGe · July 2025

1.0 Manual Labor

Everything by human effort. A PPT takes 3 days. Efficiency depends on typing speed and overtime.

2.0 Conversational AI

Humans command AI — but need to write prompts and polish every time. Faster, but repetitive.

3.0 Integrated Systems

Dedicated tools with fixed interfaces. Click instead of type. User-friendly but rigid.

4.0 Raising an AI Assistant

You train AI to know your style and habits. Then one command produces 95% of the output.

Teach → Confirm → Delegate → Feedback → Solidify. Your micro-adjustments ≤ 1%.
