Choosing AI Training? Avoid These 3 Pitfalls

YaoGe · July 2025

Most AI training sells anxiety, not capability.

Pitfall 1: Theory over practice

Employees care about one thing: can this AI save me two hours of overtime?

Pitfall 2: One-size-fits-all

AI for teaching is different from AI for sales. Good training is industry-specific.

Pitfall 3: Training ends when the session ends

AI needs practice and follow-up. Good training includes ongoing support.

3 criteria to choose

  • Needs assessment before training?
  • Hands-on practice over 50%?
  • Post-training follow-up?
